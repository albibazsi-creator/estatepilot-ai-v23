# Customer Success Playbook

The system now calculates `CustomerSuccessHealth` to help sell and retain agencies.

## Health score signals

- active listings
- published listings
- 30-day lead volume
- hot lead volume
- seller report usage
- open support tickets

## Suggested plays

- Score under 50: onboarding rescue call.
- 50–79: activate first seller report + campaign package.
- 80+: expansion offer, white-label domain, team account.
